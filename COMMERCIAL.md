# Commercial Licensing

JustPlugin is published under the **PolyForm Noncommercial License 1.0.0** (see [`LICENSE.md`](LICENSE.md)).
That means **non-commercial use is free**, but commercial use requires a separate license from the author.

## What's free under PolyForm Noncommercial

- Running JustPlugin on a Minecraft server you operate for fun, with friends, or in a community that does not generate revenue.
- Educational use — schools, courses, learning the codebase, demonstrating concepts.
- Research, experimentation, and personal study.
- Charitable, public-research, public-safety, environmental, or government use.
- Modifying the source code for any of the above purposes.
- Sharing your modifications with others under the same license, with credit preserved.

## What requires a commercial license

You **must contact the author and obtain written permission** before doing any of the following:

- Running JustPlugin on a server that earns revenue — donations toward the server itself are fine, but selling ranks, perks, or any in-game item or service that gives users an advantage requires a commercial license.
- Reselling, sublicensing, or redistributing the plugin for money in any form (one-time fee, subscription, bundle, etc.).
- Hosting the plugin as a paid service (Plugin-as-a-Service, premium hosting bundle).
- Including the plugin in a commercial product, paid mod pack, or paid server template.
- Using JustPlugin source code in another plugin or product that you sell or distribute commercially.
- Using JustPlugin's branding, logo, or trade name in any commercial context.

If you are unsure whether your use case is commercial, **assume it is and ask** — most reasonable requests are granted, and the author would rather have a quick conversation than discover misuse later.

## Credit is always required

Whether your use is commercial (with a granted license) or non-commercial, you **must give credit** to the author in any redistribution, derivative work, or product that uses JustPlugin's code:

- A visible reference to **"Jestriker"** as the original author.
- A link back to the GitHub repository: <https://github.com/Jestriker/JustPlugin>.
- The full `LICENSE` and copyright notice preserved in any distribution.

## How to request a commercial license

Contact:

- **GitHub Issues** (public): <https://github.com/Jestriker/JustPlugin/issues>
- **Discord**: <https://discord.gg/Sn5GMRKuys>

Please include:

1. Your name or organization.
2. A description of your intended use (server name, product, scope).
3. Whether the use is one-time or ongoing.
4. Approximate user reach (number of players, customers, etc.).

The author will respond with terms (which may be free, a one-time fee, or a revenue-share, depending on the case).

## Why this license model?

JustPlugin is open-source so that anyone can read, learn from, modify, and run it on their own community servers without paying a cent. At the same time, the project represents many hours of design, development, and ongoing maintenance — and shouldn't be silently bundled into someone else's paid product without the author's involvement.

This split — free for the community, paid for commerce — keeps JustPlugin sustainable and fair.

## Just want to support without a commercial license?

If your use is non-commercial but you'd still like to back the project, GitHub Sponsors works:

**→ [github.com/sponsors/Jestriker](https://github.com/sponsors/Jestriker)**

Sponsorship is **optional** under the PolyForm Noncommercial License — you don't owe anything to run the plugin on a community server. But if the plugin saves your community time and you can spare a coffee a month, it's appreciated and it directly funds future work.

Sponsorship does **not** grant a commercial license. If you need one, please follow the steps above instead.
